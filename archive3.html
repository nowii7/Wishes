THINGS I NEVER PROPERLY SAID

Every mission has a chapter that's quieter than the rest. I guess... this is ours.

When life became a little too heavy, you stayed and listened. You never had all the answers, but somehow your words always gave me something to think about. For that, thank you.

I also owe you an apology. There were times I treated you like my personal therapist, dumping endless thoughts and worries on you. Looking back, I probably talked way more than I should have. Yet you listened with patience, without making me feel judged. So... thank you for tolerating the chaos. :)

We misunderstood each other sometimes. We're different people, and maybe that's okay. Life changes people, and distances grow, but some good memories remain. I'm genuinely grateful that our paths crossed. Some people come into our lives only for a chapter, yet that chapter is still worth reading.
